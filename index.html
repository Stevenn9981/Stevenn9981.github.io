About me

My name is Wentao Ning. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong (HKU) in Dec. 2024, supervised by Prof. Reynold Cheng and Prof. Bo Tang. Before that, I received my BEng degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in Jul. 2020.

My research mainly focuses on the area of machine learning and data mining, especially recommender systems.
