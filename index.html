About me

My name is Wentao Ning. I received my BEng degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in July, 2020.

Currently, I am a first-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong (HKU) under HKU-SUSTech Joint PhD Programme, supervised by Prof. Reynold Cheng (HKU) and Prof. Bo Tang (SUSTech).

My research mainly focuses on the area of data mining and management, especially recommender system and graph neural network (GNN). I am currently working on a project about knowledge graph-based recommender system.
